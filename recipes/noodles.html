Noodles

Description

Egg noodles are, unsurprisingly, noodles made with eggs. Egg noodles have more flavor than noodles made with just flour, water, and salt.

You’ll find the full, step-by-step recipe below — but here’s a brief overview of what you can expect when you make homemade easy noodles:

Ingredients

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • ½ cup milk

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Stir flour and salt together in a large bowl. Add eggs, milk, and butter.
  3. Knead dough until smooth, about 5 minutes. Let rest in a covered bowl for 10 minutes.
  4. On a floured surface, roll dough out to a thickness of 1/8 to 1/4 inch. Cut into desired lengths and shapes.
